# Stretch Library & Coaching Rubric

Reference for prescribing sessions. Not medical advice — general mobility coaching.
Stop and defer to a clinician/PT if the user reports pain (vs. tension), numbness,
or a recent injury.

## General rules of thumb
- **Static holds** (flexibility / cool-down): 20–45s per hold, 1–3 rounds, on a
  warm body. Breathe into the stretch; never bounce.
- **Dynamic / mobility** (warm-up): controlled reps through range, 8–12 per side,
  no long holds. Use before training or when cold.
- **PNF** (contract-relax): hold ~20s, contract 5–6s, relax deeper. Advanced;
  use only on areas the user already stretches regularly.
- Stretch into mild tension, not pain. Tension that eases as you hold = good.
  Sharp, pinching, or radiating pain = back off and flag it.
- Even coverage beats obsession with one area. Rotate so nothing goes stale 7+ days.

## Progression
A stretch/area moves along: **Tight → Improving → Maintaining**.
- **Tight**: limited range, feels restricted, or newly added.
- **Improving**: range visibly increasing session over session.
- **Maintaining**: comfortable range reached; keep it with periodic touches.
Promote based on the user's self-reports and rating trend, not a fixed count.

## Library by body area

### Neck
- **Levator scapulae / upper-trap stretch** (Static) — ear toward armpit, gentle
  hand assist. Cue: keep shoulder down. Good for desk/phone tightness.
- **Chin tucks** (Mobility) — retract chin, 8–10 reps. Counters forward-head posture.

### Shoulders / Chest
- **Doorway pec stretch** (Static) — forearm on frame, step through. Cue: rotate
  away, don't shrug. Great for desk-rounded shoulders.
- **Cross-body shoulder stretch** (Static) — arm across, hold above elbow.
- **Band/stick shoulder dislocates** (Mobility) — wide grip, slow arc overhead.
- **Wall slides** (Mobility) — arms on wall, slide up/down keeping contact.

### Upper back / Thoracic
- **Thoracic rotations (open book)** (Mobility) — side-lying, rotate top arm open.
- **Cat-cow** (Mobility) — segment the spine, 8–10 slow reps. Good warm-up staple.
- **Thread the needle** (Static/Mobility) — quadruped, reach arm under.

### Lower back / Spine
- **Child's pose** (Static) — sink hips back, walk hands forward.
- **Knees-to-chest / supine twist** (Static) — gentle decompression.
- **Cat-cow** (Mobility) — also serves lower back.

### Hips / Glutes
- **90/90 hip rotations** (Mobility) — switch sides through the floor. Cornerstone
  hip drill; great for sitting-all-day tightness.
- **Couch stretch** (Static) — rear foot up on couch/wall, hip flexor + quad.
  Cue: squeeze glute, tuck pelvis. Intense — ease in.
- **Pigeon pose** (Static) — front shin across, fold forward for glute/piriformis.
- **Figure-4 stretch** (Static) — supine, ankle on knee, pull thigh in. Gentler pigeon.
- **Deep squat hold** (Mobility) — sit into bottom of squat, pry knees out.

### Hamstrings
- **Standing/seated forward fold** (Static) — hinge at hips, soft knees ok.
- **Supine hamstring (with strap)** (Static/PNF) — leg up, strap around foot.
- **Active leg swings** (Dynamic) — front-to-back, warm-up.

### Quads
- **Standing quad stretch** (Static) — heel to glute, knees together, pelvis tucked.
- **Couch stretch** (Static) — also hits quad strongly.

### Calves / Ankles
- **Wall calf stretch** (Static) — straight knee (gastroc) then bent knee (soleus).
- **Ankle rocks / knee-to-wall** (Mobility) — drive knee over toes, dorsiflexion.

### Wrists / Forearms
- **Prayer + reverse-prayer stretch** (Static) — palms together, then backs.
- **Wrist flexor/extensor stretch** (Static) — arm out, gently pull hand. Good for
  desk/keyboard and guitar players.

### Full body / Warm-up flows
- **Cat-cow → child's pose → world's greatest stretch** (Mobility) — quick full-body opener.
- **World's greatest stretch** (Mobility) — lunge + thoracic rotation + hamstring sweep.

## Quick routine templates

**Desk-break (5 min):** chin tucks → doorway pec → thoracic open book → wrist flexor/extensor.

**Post-workout cool-down (8–10 min):** target what was trained (e.g. legs day →
couch stretch + figure-4 + wall calf + forward fold), static holds 30s each.

**General mobility (12–15 min):** cat-cow warm-up → 90/90 hips → couch stretch →
pigeon/figure-4 → hamstring fold → thoracic rotations.

**Lower-back relief (6–8 min):** child's pose → knees-to-chest → supine twist →
figure-4 → cat-cow.
